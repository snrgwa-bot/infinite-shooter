PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: March 5, 2026

INTRODUCTION

Essential Dev Team ("we", "our", or "us") built Infinite Cosmic Shooter as a free app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from our users. The app does not require account creation or login.

All game progress (scores, coins, upgrades, settings) is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage and is never transmitted to our servers.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Unity Ads — Used to display optional reward advertisements. Unity may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with their own privacy policy. See: Unity Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit — Used to process in-app purchases. Purchases are handled entirely by Apple in accordance with their privacy policy. See: Apple Privacy Policy

IN-APP PURCHASES

The app offers optional in-app purchases for virtual coins. All transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. The app does not require any personal data to function. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

DATA RETENTION

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no data to retain. Game data stored on your device can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

YOUR RIGHTS

As we do not collect personal data, there is typically no data subject to access, correction, or deletion requests. However, if you have any concerns, feel free to contact us.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
