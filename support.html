FAQ

How do I unlock new ships?
Progress through waves and earn coins to purchase new ships from the in-game Store. Each ship has unique stats and abilities.
How do upgrades work?
Upgrades are per-ship. Visit the Store and spend coins to improve fire rate, bullet power, shield duration, and more. Each ship maintains its own upgrade levels.
Can I restore my purchases?
Yes! In-app purchases can be restored automatically. Open the Store, go to the Coins tab, and tap "Restore Purchases".
The game is running slow. What can I do?
Try closing other apps running in the background. The game runs best on iPhone 8 and newer. If the issue persists, please contact us.
Is an internet connection required?
No! The core game works fully offline. An internet connection is only needed for watching reward ads and making in-app purchases.
